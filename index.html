Career:

Here's a resume about Boseman's career:

  • 2000-2007:
    • Named Drama League Directing Fellow in 2000, he directed George C. Wolfe's "The Colored Museum"
    • He won an AUDELCO award in 2002.
    • As member of the National Shakespeare Company of New York, he played Romeo in Romeo and Juliet and Malcom in Macbeth
    • In 2003 he made his first appearence on tv in an episode of "Third Watch", also he was part of some episodes of "Law & Order", "Cold Case", "CSI: NY" and "ER"
    • Deep Azure, his best-known play, was commissioned in 2004 and it was nominated for a Jeff Award in 2006

  • 2008-2015:
    • In 2008 he appeared in his first feature film, "The Express: The Ernie Davis Story"
    • In 2010 he landed his first regular role in the television series "Persons Unknown"
    • Boseman's breakthrough role came with the film "42" in 2013, he was picked for the role because of his bravery, he chose one of the most difficult scenes
    • His performance was praised in the film "Get on Up", on Rotten Tomatoes was a crtitical quote that said: "With an unforgettable Chadwick Boseman in the starring role, Get On Up offers the Godfather of Soul a fittingly dynamic homage."

  • 2016-2019
    • In 2016, Boseman began portraying the Marvel Comics character The Black Panther in Captain America Civil War. A curious fact is that instead of auditioning for the role, they had a discussion about what they want to do and what did he thought about it
    • A couple of years later, he return in "The Black Panther" movie. The film that was critically well-received, so many people praised him for his perfomance
    • Also he participated in Avengers Infinity War and Avengers Endgame, with Endgame being the highest-grossing film of all time
    • Boseman's last physical appearance as The Black Panther was at the Tony Stark's funeral (Endgame scene)
  • 2020:
    • In 2019 Boseman was announced as part of the cast for the serie "Da 5 Bloods"
    • In 2020 he was included on the 10 Best Movie Performances. He was also nominated in the Best Actor category at the Academy Awards, British Academy Film Awards, Golden Globe Awards and Screen Actors Guild Awards
    • His last film before he died was "Ma Rainey's Black Bottom". He was excited for this role because it was a difficult and callenging role
